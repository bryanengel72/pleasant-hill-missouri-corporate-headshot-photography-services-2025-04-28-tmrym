Frequently Asked Questions

What should I wear for my headshot session?

We recommend solid colors and professional attire that reflects your industry standards. Avoid busy patterns and bright colors that might distract from your face. We'll provide a detailed preparation guide upon booking.

How long does a typical session take?

Standard sessions last 45-60 minutes, allowing time for multiple poses, outfits, and lighting setups. We ensure a relaxed atmosphere to capture your best expressions.

What's included in the final delivery?

You'll receive a selection of fully retouched, high-resolution images optimized for both print and digital use, including LinkedIn-specific crops and web-ready versions.

How quickly will I receive my photos?

You'll receive your professionally edited photos within 3-5 business days. Rush delivery is available for an additional fee.

Do you offer group or team photo sessions?

Yes, we specialize in corporate team photography and can accommodate groups of any size. We offer special packages for companies needing multiple headshots.
